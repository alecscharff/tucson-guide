Don't-Miss Checklist

Top Attractions

  • Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

    Not a typical museum - mostly outdoors! 230+ animal species including mountain lions, black bears, wolves, prairie dogs, and hummingbirds. The Packrat Playhouse is a 4,000 sq ft indoor play space perfect for ages 6 & 9.

  • Sabino Canyon

    Electric tram takes you 3.7 miles into stunning Santa Catalina Mountain canyon with narrated tour. Kids can splash in seasonal creek pools. Option to ride up and hike back down.

  • Reid Park Zoo

    250+ animals on 24 manageable acres. Not overwhelming for younger kids. Features elephants, lions, giraffes, with giraffe feeding opportunities and conservation carousel.

  • Saguaro National Park

    Home to the nation's largest cacti - the iconic giant saguaro. Two districts offer scenic drives, easy trails, and Junior Ranger programs. Quintessential Arizona experience.

  • Mini Time Machine Museum

    500+ miniature displays where kids pretend they're giants! Enchanted realm with dragons and fairies, history galleries, whimsical scenes. Totally unique experience.

Family-Friendly Restaurants

  • Bobo's Restaurant

    Classic diner known for delicious breakfast and lunch. Great for picky eaters with grilled cheese, hamburgers, and fries.

  • El Guero Canelo

    Famous for Sonoran hot dogs, a unique Tucson culinary experience. Fast, casual, and a hit with kids.
