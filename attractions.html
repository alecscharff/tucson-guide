Tucson Attractions

16 family-friendly activities rated for kid appeal (ages 6 & 9)

5 Stars (Must-Do)

Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Zoo + Museum + Gardens

World-renowned outdoor museum with 230+ animal species. Packrat Playhouse indoor play space, hummingbird aviary, mountain lions, bears, wolves.

5 Stars 3-4 hours $78 family
View Details

Sabino Canyon

Tram Ride + Nature

Scenic desert canyon with narrated electric tram ride. Creek pools for splashing, hiking trails. CLOSED Dec 25 & Jan 1.

5 Stars 2-4 hours $54 family
View Details

4 Stars (Highly Recommended)

Reid Park Zoo

Family Zoo

250+ animals on 24 acres. Perfect size for younger kids. Elephants, lions, giraffes, giraffe feeding, conservation carousel.

4 Stars 2-2.5 hours $37 family
View Details

Saguaro National Park

Giant Cacti

Iconic giant saguaro cacti. Two districts (West & East). Junior Ranger program, scenic drives, Desert Discovery Nature Trail.

4 Stars 2-3 hours $25 vehicle
View Details

Mini Time Machine Museum

Miniatures Museum

500+ miniature displays. Kids pretend they're giants! Enchanted realm with fantasy worlds, dragons, fairies. Captivating for both ages.

4 Stars 1.5-2 hours $48 family
View Details

Best Sunset Spots

See our full Guide to Tucson Sunsets.

Gates Pass

Sunset Viewpoint

Mountain pass with panoramic desert views. USA Today "Best Place to Watch a Sunset" winner. Sunset ~5:20 PM in December.

Sunset 30-60 min FREE
View Details

Sentinel Peak ("A" Mountain)

City Views

Small peak with iconic "A" for University of Arizona. 360-degree views of Tucson. Easy drive-up access.

Sunset 30 min FREE
View Details

Windy Point Vista

Mountain Views

Halfway up Mt. Lemmon. Sweeping views of the valley from 6,600 ft. Dramatic rock formations.

Sunset 45 min FREE
View Details

3 Stars (Good Additions)

Mt. Lemmon

Mountain Village

27-mile scenic drive through 5 climate zones. Summerhaven village at top, possible snow in winter. Check road conditions first!

3 Stars 4-6 hours FREE
View Details

Children's Museum Tucson

Indoor Play

Hands-on museum with interactive exhibits. Age 6 may enjoy more than age 9. FREE Thursday evenings 5-7 PM!

3 Stars 2-3 hours $44 family
View Details

San Xavier Mission

Historic Church

"White Dove of the Desert" - stunning 18th-century Spanish colonial mission. Beautiful architecture, free docent tours, fry bread vendors.

3 Stars 30-60 min FREE
View Details

Trail Dust Town

Western Theme Park

Recreated 1880s Old West town. FREE admission! Wild West stunt shows (Fri-Sun), mini train, ferris wheel, gold panning. Evening-focused.

3 Stars 1-2 hours FREE + rides
View Details

Horseback Riding

Desert Trail Rides

Several options for ages 6+. Tucson Mountain Stables, Tattered Saddle, Catalina Riding Club. 1-2 hour rides through Sonoran Desert.

3 Stars 1-2 hours $50-80/person

Second Sky

Indoor Play Space

Indoor play space for kids. Open play Dec 29-30, 9 AM - 2 PM. Great for last day before leaving town!
