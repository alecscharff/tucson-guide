Daily Itinerary Ideas

Pre-planned days combining attractions, dining, and downtime

These ready-made itineraries help you make the most of your Tucson visit. Each day balances activities with rest time and includes meal recommendations. Mix and match based on your interests and energy levels!

Recommended

Day 1: Desert Museum + Saguaro + Sunset

The Iconic Tucson Experience

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging Breakfast at home
10:00 AM Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum 30 min drive | Arrive at opening
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM Explore museum Animals, Packrat Playhouse, trails
1:00 PM Lunch at Ironwood Terraces On-site restaurant | Kids menu $9
1:45 - 2:30 PM Finish exploring / gift shop -
2:45 PM Saguaro National Park (West) 5 min drive | Junior Ranger program
3:00 - 4:00 PM Scenic loop drive + short trail Amazing saguaro photos!
4:15 PM Gates Pass 10 min drive
4:30 - 5:30 PM Watch sunset Sunset around 5:20 PM in December
6:00 PM Dinner at El Guero Canelo Quick, casual Sonoran food

Cost: ~$103 (Desert Museum $78 + Saguaro $25)

Why This Works: Combines the best desert experiences in one efficient loop. All locations are close together on the west side of Tucson.

Day 2: Zoo + Mercado + Trail Dust Town

Balanced Family Day

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging -
9:45 AM Reid Park Zoo 15 min drive
9:45 AM - 12:15 PM Explore zoo Giraffes, playground, carousel
12:30 PM Lunch at Seis Kitchen At Mercado San Agustin, 15 min drive
1:30 - 3:00 PM Browse Mercado + shops La Estrella Bakery, local artisans
3:30 - 5:00 PM REST - Pool / lodging Downtime essential!
5:30 PM Trail Dust Town 15 min drive | Free admission
5:30 - 7:30 PM Wild West stunt show + rides Train, ferris wheel, show at 7 PM
7:30 PM Dinner on-site or nearby Pinnacle Peak Steakhouse at Trail Dust

Cost: ~$49 (Zoo $37 + Trail Dust rides ~$12)

Why This Works: Manageable morning activity, shopping for adults, essential rest time, then fun evening entertainment for kids.

Day 3: Sabino Canyon Morning

Active Outdoor Day

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging -
10:00 AM Sabino Canyon 20 min drive | Book tram ahead!
10:15 AM Board tram Narrated ride into canyon
10:15 - 11:15 AM Tram ride up canyon Scenic, educational
11:15 AM - 1:00 PM Explore at top / splash in creek Bring towels for water play!
1:00 PM Tram back down (or hike) -
2:00 PM Late lunch Prep & Pastry or Jewel's Noodle
3:00 - 5:00 PM REST - Pool / lodging Essential after active morning
5:30 PM Dinner Easy, casual option

Cost: ~$54 (Tram + parking)

Why This Works: Morning is coolest for outdoor activity, afternoon rest after active tram/hiking, flexible dinner.
