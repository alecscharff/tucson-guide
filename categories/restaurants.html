Dining Tips for Families

  • Lunch is easier: Faster service, less crowded than dinner
  • Patios are common: December weather is perfect for outdoor dining
  • Mexican basics: Most places can make cheese quesadilla even if not on menu
  • Call ahead: Confirm hours (holidays vary) and specific kids menu items
  • Health scores: Look up at healthinspect.pima.gov
