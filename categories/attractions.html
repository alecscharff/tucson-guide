Planning Your Attraction Schedule

  • Morning is best: Desert attractions are cooler before noon
  • December weather: Perfect! Highs 60s-70s, ideal for outdoor activities
  • Build in downtime: Don't over-schedule - kids need pool/rest breaks
  • Combine nearby: Desert Museum + Saguaro + Gates Pass work perfectly together
  • Junior Ranger programs: Available at Saguaro National Park

Geographic Clusters

West Side (Tucson Mountains):

  • Desert Museum + Gates Pass (8 min apart)
  • Saguaro National Park West (5 min from Desert Museum)
  • Sentinel Peak (20 min)

Northeast Side:

  • Sabino Canyon + Mt. Lemmon (same direction, 40 min apart)

Central/Downtown:

  • Reid Park Zoo + Children's Museum (8 min apart)
  • Mercado San Agustin + Downtown (all within 15 min)
