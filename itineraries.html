Daily Itinerary Ideas

10 pre-planned day options - Mix and match for your perfect trip!

How to Use These Itineraries

You have 4 full days in Tucson (Dec 26-29), but we've created 10 itinerary options so you can mix and match based on your interests, weather, and energy levels. Each itinerary is geographically grouped to minimize driving time.

Pro tip: Book Sabino Canyon tram tickets in advance, and remember it's CLOSED Dec 25 and Jan 1.

Option 1: West Side Adventure (S-TIER DAY)

Desert Museum + Saguaro National Park + Gates Pass Sunset

The iconic Tucson experience - all in the same area!

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging
10:00 AM Arrive Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum 30 min drive
10:00 AM-1:00 PM Explore Desert Museum Animals, Packrat Playhouse, exhibits
1:00 PM Lunch at Ironwood Terraces Kids menu $9 - burger, grilled cheese, pizza
1:45-2:30 PM Finish exploring / gift shop
2:45 PM Drive to Saguaro National Park West 5 min
3:00-4:00 PM Scenic loop drive + Desert Discovery Trail Junior Ranger program!
4:15 PM Drive to Gates Pass 10 min
4:30-5:30 PM Watch sunset at Gates Pass Sunset ~5:20 PM
6:00 PM Dinner at Seis Kitchen (Mercado) or El Guero Canelo

Pro tip: Stop at David Yetman West Trailhead first for variety, then Gates Pass Overlook for sunset (NYT recommendation).

Cost: ~$78 (Desert Museum) + $25 (Saguaro) = ~$103

Option 2: Sabino Canyon Day (S-TIER)

Tram Ride + Creek Splashing + Nearby Lunch

Active morning in the Santa Catalina Mountains

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging
10:00 AM Arrive Sabino Canyon 20 min drive
10:15 AM Board tram Book ahead at sabinocanyoncrawler.com!
10:15-11:15 AM Tram ride up canyon Narrated, scenic
11:15 AM-1:00 PM Explore at top / splash in creek Bring towels!
1:00 PM Tram back down (or hike if energetic)
2:00 PM Late lunch at Prep & Pastry Grilled cheese + fries, nearby
3:00-5:00 PM REST - Pool / lodging downtime
5:30 PM Easy dinner near lodging

Note: CLOSED December 25 and January 1

Cost: ~$54 (tram + parking)

Option 4: Mt. Lemmon Day Trip

Full Day Mountain Adventure

Only if weather is good - call 520-351-3351 first

Time Activity Notes
9:30 AM Leave lodging Check road conditions first!
10:30 AM Arrive Summerhaven (top) 60 min scenic drive through 5 climate zones
10:30 AM-12:30 PM Explore village, play in snow (if any) Fudge at General Store!
12:30 PM Lunch at Iron Door Restaurant Family-friendly
1:30 PM Scenic drive back with stops Multiple viewpoints
3:00 PM Return to Tucson
3:30-5:30 PM REST - Pool / downtime Let sore muscles recover!
6:00 PM Dinner near lodging

Note: Mt. Lemmon is its own day - don't try to combine with other attractions.

Cost: FREE (no entrance fee)

Option 9: Horseback Riding Day

Morning Ride + Relaxed Afternoon

Special desert experience for the family

Time Activity Notes
8:30 AM Leave lodging
9:00 AM Horseback ride Book ahead! Options: Tucson Mountain Stables ($50-80/person), Tattered Saddle (ages 6+)
11:00 AM Finish ride
11:30 AM Lunch at nearby restaurant
1:00-4:00 PM REST - Pool / lodging Let sore muscles recover!
4:30 PM Playground or easy activity
6:00 PM Dinner

Horseback options for 6-year-olds:

  • Tucson Mountain Stables - 1hr $50, 1.5hr $70, family-owned
  • Tattered Saddle - 45min "Conquistador Experience" ages 6+
  • Catalina Riding Club - Lessons + trail rides, sunset options

Cost: $50-80 per person (1-1.5 hr ride)
