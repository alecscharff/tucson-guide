Saguaro National Park

4 Stars | Iconic Giant Cacti & Scenic Drives

Home / Attractions / Saguaro National Park

Overview

Tier Rating: 4 STARS (Highly Recommended)

Kid Appeal: 4/5 stars

Home to the nation's largest cacti - the iconic giant saguaro. The park is split into two districts: West (Tucson Mountain District, near Desert Museum) and East (Rincon Mountain District). Thousands of saguaros, scenic drives, and easy hikes.

Key Details

  • West District: Near Desert Museum - sunrise to sunset
  • East District: 5 AM - 8:30 PM (vehicle gate)
  • Cost: $25 per vehicle (7-day pass, both districts)
  • Time Needed: 2-3 hours per district
  • Distance from Rillito Park: West: 18 miles, 35 min | East: 15 miles, 25 min

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Junior Ranger Program - free activity book, earn a badge!
  • Desert Discovery Nature Trail (West) - paved, under 1 mile, amazing saguaro photos
  • Easy scenic drives with pull-offs for photos
  • Visitor centers have cactus candy and souvenirs

Pro Tips

  • West district pairs perfectly with Desert Museum (5 min apart)
  • Best in morning when cooler
  • December weather is ideal (60s-70s)
  • America the Beautiful pass accepted
  • Don't miss: Signal Hill petroglyphs (West), Cactus Forest Drive (East)
