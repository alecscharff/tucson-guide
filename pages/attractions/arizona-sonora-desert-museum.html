Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Zoo + Botanical Garden + Museum

Kid Appeal: ★★★★★

Overview

A world-renowned combination zoo, natural history museum, and botanical garden showcasing the Sonoran Desert. Not a typical museum - mostly outdoors! This is THE must-do Tucson experience.

Highlights

  • 230+ animal species including mountain lions, black bears, wolves, javelinas, prairie dogs
  • Packrat Playhouse - 4,000 sq ft indoor play space (ages 0-12)
  • Hummingbird aviary with hundreds of hummingbirds
  • Animal encounters and feeding demonstrations
  • Stunning desert botanical gardens
  • On-site restaurant with kids menu

Essential Information

Address: 2021 N. Kinney Rd, Tucson AZ 85743

Hours: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM (October-May)

Cost: Adults $24.95, Kids (3-12) $13.95, Under 3 FREE

Parking: FREE

Time Needed: 3-4 hours minimum

Distance from Rillito Park: 16 miles, 30 min from Rillito Park

For Kids (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Packrat Playhouse: Slide down a gila monster tail, climb through cactus pads, explore tunnels (10 AM - 4 PM)
  • Animal encounters throughout the day
  • Junior docent programs
  • Interactive exhibits at every turn

Food On-Site

Ironwood Terraces - Kids Menu $9: Choice of hamburger, cheese pizza, hot dog, or grilled cheese + fries or fruit

Pro Tips

  • Arrive at opening (8:30 AM) to see animals most active
  • Morning is cooler - desert heats up by afternoon
  • Combine with Gates Pass sunset (only 5 min drive)
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Bring sunscreen, hats, water
