Horseback Riding in Tucson

3 Stars | Desert Trail Rides

Home / Attractions / Horseback Riding

Overview

Tier Rating: 3 STARS (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 5/5 stars

Several options for horseback riding with kids ages 6+. Great way to experience the Sonoran Desert landscape.

Key Details

  • Time Needed: 1-2 hours
  • Cost: $50-80 per person
  • Age: Most rides accept kids 6+ years old

Options for 6-Year-Olds

Tattered Saddle

  • Children ages 6+ welcome on most rides
  • Hand-led "pony rides" for nervous kids
  • "Conquistador Horse Experience" - 45 min, ages 6+
  • Helmets provided free for children (required)

Tucson Mountain Stables

  • Family-owned, all ages welcome
  • 1 HR ride $50/person | 1.5 HR $70 | 2 HR $80
  • Sunset ride $75 (1.5 HR)
  • Private rides (just your group)
  • Note: Prices increase January 1, 2026

Catalina Riding Club (Westward Look Resort)

  • Private/group lessons for ages 6+
  • Kinder lessons for ages 2-5
  • Trail rides at sunrise (7am) and sunset (6:30pm)
  • 60-90 min rides include safety lesson

All Around Trail Horses

  • 90 min to 4-hour rides
  • "Perfect for the little ones!"
  • Friendly wranglers, beginner-friendly

White Stallion Ranch

Note: White Stallion Ranch is primarily for overnight guests (3-night minimum stay). Horseback riding is included for guests but not available as a day activity for non-guests.

Pro Tips

  • Call ahead to confirm age requirements
  • Book in advance, especially for sunset rides
  • Wear closed-toe shoes, long pants recommended
  • Bring water, sunscreen, hat
