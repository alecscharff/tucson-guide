Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

S-Tier | Zoo + Botanical Garden + Museum - The Ultimate Desert Experience

Home / Attractions / Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Overview

A world-renowned zoo, natural history museum, and botanical garden showcasing the Sonoran Desert. 230+ animal species including mountain lions, black bears, wolves, javelinas, prairie dogs, and hummingbirds. Not a typical museum - mostly outdoors!

S-Tier Must-Do Kid Appeal: 5 stars 3-4 hours $78 family of 4

For Kids (Ages 6 & 9)

Packrat Playhouse

4,000 sq ft indoor play space perfect for ages 0-12:

  • Slide down a gila monster tail
  • Climb through cactus pads
  • Explore tunnels and interactive exhibits
  • Hours: 10 AM - 4 PM, included with admission

Other Kid Favorites

  • Animal encounters and feeding demonstrations
  • Hummingbird aviary - walk among hundreds of hummingbirds
  • Prairie dog exhibit (kids love these!)
  • See mountain lions, black bears, and wolves up close

Food On-Site

Ironwood Terraces - Kids Menu: $9

Choice of: hamburger, cheese pizza, hot dog, or grilled cheese

Choice of: french fries or fruit

Great option so you don't have to leave for lunch!

Essential Details

Address: 2021 N. Kinney Rd, Tucson AZ 85743
Hours: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM (Oct-May)
Cost: Adults $24.95, Kids (3-12) $13.95, Under 3 FREE
Family of 4: ~$78
Parking: FREE
Time Needed: 3-4 hours minimum
Distance from Rillito: 16 miles, 30 min drive

Pro Tips

  • Arrive at opening (8:30 AM) to see animals most active
  • Morning is cooler - desert heats up by afternoon
  • Combine with Gates Pass sunset (only 5 min drive)
  • Can also pair with Saguaro National Park West (5 min away)
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Bring sunscreen, hats, water
