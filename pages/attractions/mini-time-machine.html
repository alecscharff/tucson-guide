Mini Time Machine Museum of Miniatures

A-Tier | Magical Miniature Worlds

Home / Attractions / Mini Time Machine

Overview

Tier Rating: A-TIER (Highly Recommended)

Kid Appeal: 5/5 stars

A magical museum with 500+ miniature displays - kids pretend they're giants looking into tiny worlds! Enchanted realm, history galleries, and whimsical scenes. Totally unique.

Key Details

  • Address: 4455 E Camp Lowell Dr, Tucson AZ 85712
  • Hours: Tue-Sun 9 AM - 4 PM, CLOSED Mondays
  • Cost: Adults $15, Kids (4-17) $9, Under 3 FREE
  • Parking: FREE
  • Time Needed: 1.5-2 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 4 miles, 10 min

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Enchanted Realm - fantasy worlds, dragons, fairies
  • Exploring the World - tiny scenes from different eras/places
  • Stools provided so shorter kids can see everything
  • Captivating for both ages - detailed tiny worlds spark imagination

Pro Tips

  • Great for a hot afternoon or morning activity
  • Less crowded than major attractions
  • Near R11 Piazza Gavi for lunch
  • Allow kids to explore at their own pace
  • Don't rush - the details are amazing
