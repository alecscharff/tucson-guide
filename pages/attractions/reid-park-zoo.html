Reid Park Zoo

4 Stars | Perfect Family-Sized Zoo

Home / Attractions / Reid Park Zoo

Overview

Tier Rating: 4 STARS (Highly Recommended)

Kid Appeal: 4/5 stars

A smaller, manageable zoo with 250+ animals on 24 acres. Less overwhelming than large zoos - perfect for younger kids. Features elephants, lions, giraffes, and more.

Key Details

  • Address: 3400 E Zoo Ct, Tucson AZ 85716
  • Hours: 9 AM - 4 PM (last entry 3:30 PM)
  • Cost: Adults $11.25, Kids (2-14) $7.00, Under 2 FREE
  • Parking: FREE
  • Time Needed: 2-2.5 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 6 miles, 15 min

CLOSURE

Closed December 25 (Christmas Day) and Thanksgiving

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Right-sized for ages 6 & 9 (not overwhelming)
  • Giraffe feeding opportunities
  • Playground areas
  • Conservation Carousel

Pro Tips

  • Pairs well with Mercado San Agustin for lunch (15 min drive)
  • Good option for a shorter/easier day
  • Can do morning zoo + afternoon Children's Museum
