San Xavier del Bac Mission

3 Stars | "White Dove of the Desert"

Home / Attractions / San Xavier Mission

Overview

Tier Rating: 3 STARS (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 2/5 stars

"The White Dove of the Desert" - a stunning 18th-century Spanish colonial mission. Active Catholic church on the Tohono O'odham reservation. Beautiful architecture and art.

Key Details

  • Address: 1950 W San Xavier Rd
  • Hours: 9 AM - 4 PM daily
  • Tours: Free docent tours Mon-Sat, 9:15 AM - 12:15 PM (hourly)
  • Cost: FREE (donations welcome)
  • Time Needed: 30-60 minutes
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 12 miles, 20-25 min

What Kids Will Love

  • Lighting a candle (make a donation)
  • Climbing "Grotto Hill" next door (Great sunset spot!)
  • Fry bread with honey!

Pro Tips

  • Quick stop - doesn't need to be a half-day
  • Pairs well with another activity
  • Morning visit to catch free docent tour
