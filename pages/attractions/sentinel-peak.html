Sentinel Peak ("A" Mountain)

B-Tier | Iconic "A" Viewpoint

Home / Attractions / Sentinel Peak

Overview

Tier Rating: B-TIER (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 3/5 stars

A small peak on Tucson's west side with the iconic "A" (for University of Arizona). Offers 360-degree views of Tucson and surrounding mountains. Easy drive-up access.

Key Details

  • Address: 1001 S Sentinel Peak Rd
  • Hours: Sunrise - Sunset
  • Vehicle Access: Mon: NONE | Tue-Thu: 2 PM-sunset | Fri-Sun: 11 AM-sunset
  • Cost: FREE
  • Time Needed: 30 min
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 6 miles, 12 min

NOTICE

Road may be closed Nov 17 - mid December 2024 for construction. Verify before visiting.

What Kids Will Love

  • Drive to the top for easy access
  • Short walking trails available
  • City lights view if staying past sunset

Pro Tips

  • Alternative to Gates Pass (closer, but less dramatic)
  • Good for quick sunset if short on time
  • Check road closure status before visiting
