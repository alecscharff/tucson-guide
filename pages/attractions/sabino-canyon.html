Sabino Canyon Recreation Area

S-Tier Must-Do | Nature + Tram Ride

Home / Attractions / Sabino Canyon

Overview

Tier Rating: S-TIER (Must-Do)

Kid Appeal: 5/5 stars

A scenic desert canyon in the Santa Catalina Mountains with a narrated tram ride, hiking trails, and seasonal creek pools. The electric tram takes you 3.7 miles into the canyon with 9 stops.

Key Details

  • Address: 5700 N Sabino Canyon Rd, Tucson AZ 85750
  • Tram Hours: 9 AM - 4 PM (every 30-60 min)
  • Cost: Tram: Adults $15, Kids (3-12) $8 | Amenity Fee: $8 per vehicle
  • Time Needed: 2-4 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 10 miles, 20 min

IMPORTANT

  • CLOSED December 25 (Christmas Day) and January 1
  • Buy tickets in advance at sabinocanyoncrawler.com
  • Arrive 15 min before departure; 30 min extra on busy days

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Narrated tram ride (1 hour round trip) - educational and scenic
  • Option to ride up and hike back down (great for energetic kids)
  • Sabino Dam area has calm water for splashing (bring towels!)
  • See desert wildlife, giant saguaros, rock formations

Family-Friendly Hike Options

Option Description Best For
Tram only Ride up and back Easy, any age
Tram up, walk down 3.7 mile paved road Active families
Sabino Dam area Short walk from early stops Splashing in water

Pro Tips

  • Best months: September - April (your visit is perfect!)
  • No dogs allowed
  • Pack water, snacks, sun protection
  • Water shoes useful if planning to wade
  • Morning is less crowded
