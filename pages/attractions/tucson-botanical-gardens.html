Tucson Botanical Gardens

Butterfly Pavilion & Model Trains

Kid Appeal: ★★★★☆

Overview

A 5.5-acre collection of 17 specialty gardens in the heart of Tucson. Known for its intimate scale, shade, and seasonal exhibits like Butterfly Magic.

Highlights

  • Butterfly Magic: Tropical greenhouse with hundreds of butterflies (Oct-May).
  • Wanderland: Massive outdoor G-scale model train layout.
  • Children's Discovery Garden.
  • Edna's Eatery on-site cafe.

Essential Information

Address: 2150 N Alvernon Way, Tucson, AZ 85712

Cost: ~$58 for a family of 4.

Time Needed: 1.5 - 2 hours

For Kids

Kids love chasing (but not touching!) the butterflies in the pavilion. The model trains are a huge hit, winding through the plants.
