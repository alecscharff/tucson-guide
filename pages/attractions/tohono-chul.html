Tohono Chul

Nature Trails & Children's Museum Branch

Kid Appeal: ★★★★☆

Overview

Recognized as one of the world's great botanical gardens, Tohono Chul ("Desert Corner") offers a relaxed setting with winding trails, art galleries, and a bistro.

Highlights

  • Children's Museum Branch: A dedicated "Nature Ninos" outdoor play area managed by the Children's Museum Tucson.
  • Nature Trails: Easy, flat paths winding through saguaros and desert flora.
  • Hummingbird Gardens: Attracting many local species.
  • Bistro: Highly rated on-site dining (great for brunch).

Essential Information

Address: 7366 N Paseo Del Norte, Tucson, AZ 85704

Cost: ~$45 for a family of 4.

Time Needed: 1.5 - 2.5 hours

For Kids

The collaboration with the Children's Museum means there are specific activities designed for little ones, unlike typical "look but don't touch" gardens.
