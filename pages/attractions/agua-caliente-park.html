Agua Caliente Park

Natural Oasis with Ponds

Kid Appeal: ★★★☆☆

Overview

A unique desert oasis featuring a warm spring that feeds into ponds lined with massive palm trees. It's a striking contrast to the surrounding desert and a historic site.

Highlights

  • The Ponds: Home to turtles, fish, and ducks.
  • Walking Paths: Paved and unpaved trails around the water.
  • Wildlife: Excellent for birdwatching.
  • Picnic Areas: Great spots under the shade of palms.

Essential Information

Address: 12325 E Roger Rd, Tucson, AZ 85749

Cost: FREE

Time Needed: 1 - 1.5 hours

For Kids

Kids love spotting the turtles sunning themselves on logs and seeing the ducks. It's a very relaxed environment perfect for a picnic lunch.
