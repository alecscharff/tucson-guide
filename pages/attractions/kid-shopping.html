Kid-Friendly Shopping

Toy Stores, Bookshops & Children's Boutiques

Home / Attractions / Kid-Friendly Shopping

Overview

Tucson has several excellent toy stores, children's bookshops, and specialty stores that kids will love. Here are the best options organized by category.

Source: Tucson.com - Best Kid Stores

Toy & Book Stores

Mildred & Dildred

Address: 1725 N. Swan Road

Classic Tucson toy shop with a huge selection of toys, games, puzzles, and children's books. A local institution!

View on Google Maps

Littlest Bookshop

Address: 5011 E. Fifth St.

Dedicated children's bookstore specializing in kids' literature. Great for book lovers!

View on Google Maps

Bricks & Minifigs

Address: 6145 E. Broadway

LEGO heaven! New and pre-owned LEGO sets, plus individual minifigures and bricks. Kids can spend hours here.

View on Google Maps

Children's Clothing & Baby

Little Bird Nesting Company

Address: 4508 E. Broadway

New and gently-used children's clothing boutique. Great prices on quality kids' clothes.

View on Google Maps

The Nest

Address: 6370 N. Campbell Ave.

Baby and children's boutique with clothing, toys, pacifiers, and accessories. Great for gifts!

View on Google Maps

Once Upon A Child

Address: 6252 E. Speedway

Buy and sell gently used kids' clothing, toys, and baby gear. Great for budget-conscious families!

View on Google Maps

Minimal Mae

Address: 160 S. Avenida del Convento (Mercado District)

Sustainably-made children's clothing, toys, and accessories. Located at Mercado San Agustin!

View on Google Maps

Cactus Wren Kids

Website: cactuswrenkids.com

Desert-themed children's clothing, accessories, and toys. Adorable Southwest-inspired designs!

Specialty & Pop Culture

Kira Kira Collectibles

Address: 2903 E. Speedway

K-pop and anime merchandise including plushies, figurines, and collectibles. Great for tweens!

View on Google Maps

KpopNJoy

Address: 4813 E. Speedway

K-pop shop with music, snacks, and Korean beauty items. Another good option for tweens!

View on Google Maps

Heroes and Villains

Address: 4533 E. Broadway

Comic shop operating for two decades with games, vinyl, and collectibles. Great for comic-loving kids!

View on Google Maps

Pro Tips

  • Mildred & Dildred is the classic must-visit for toy shopping
  • Bricks & Minifigs is a hit with LEGO fans - budget extra time!
  • The K-pop stores are great for kids ages 9+
  • Minimal Mae at Mercado San Agustin pairs well with lunch at Seis Kitchen
