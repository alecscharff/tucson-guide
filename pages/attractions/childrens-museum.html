Children's Museum Tucson

3 Stars | Indoor Play & Learning

Home / Attractions / Children's Museum

Overview

Tier Rating: 3 STARS (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 4/5 stars

A hands-on children's museum with interactive exhibits designed for younger kids. Good backup for hot afternoon or rainy day (rare in December).

Key Details

  • Address: 200 S 6th Ave (Downtown)
  • Hours: Tue-Sun 9 AM - 5 PM | Thu until 7 PM | Closed Monday
  • Cost: $11 per person, Under 12 months FREE
  • Parking: Metered street ($1/2 hrs, FREE weekends)
  • Time Needed: 2-3 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 5 miles, 15 min

Discounts

  • FREE Thursday evenings 5-7 PM (Discovery Nights)
  • Military: 1 free child per ID
  • Pima County Library card: 2 free admissions (Act One Culture Pass)

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Interactive play exhibits
  • Age 6 may enjoy more than age 9 (skews younger)
  • Good energy-burning option

Pro Tips

  • Combine with downtown lunch
  • Thursday evening free admission is great value
  • Nearby: Hotel Congress, downtown restaurants
