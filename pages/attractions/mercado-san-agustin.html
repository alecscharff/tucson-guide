Mercado San Agustin

B-Tier | Food & Shopping Courtyard

Home / Attractions / Mercado San Agustin

Overview

Tier Rating: B-TIER (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 3/5 stars

An open-air community gathering place with restaurants, shops, and a farmers market. Good spot for lunch or casual browsing.

Key Details

  • Address: 100 S Avenida del Convento, Tucson AZ 85701
  • Hours: Varies by vendor (most open 8 AM - 8 PM)
  • Farmers Market: Saturday 8 AM - 12 PM (year-round)
  • Cost: FREE to walk around, purchases vary
  • Parking: FREE lot
  • Time Needed: 1-2 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 6 miles, 15 min

Restaurants & Shops

  • Seis Kitchen - Regional Mexican (see restaurant page)
  • Agustin Kitchen - Farm-to-table dining
  • MSA Annex - Coffee and pastries
  • Minimal Mae - Sustainably-made children's clothing, toys, and accessories (160 S. Avenida del Convento)
  • Local artisan shops
  • Community events and live music

Source: Tucson.com - Best Kid Stores

What Kids Will Love

  • Open courtyard to walk around
  • Saturday farmers market
  • Multiple food options
  • Outdoor seating and casual vibe

Pro Tips

  • Great lunch stop paired with Zoo or Children's Museum
  • Saturday morning farmers market is lively
  • Check event calendar for special happenings
  • Outdoor seating - great in December weather
