Mt. Lemmon

B-Tier | Scenic Mountain Drive

Home / Attractions / Mt Lemmon

Overview

Tier Rating: B-TIER (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 4/5 stars (if snow)

A 27-mile scenic drive from desert floor to 9,000+ feet, passing through 5 climate zones. The town of Summerhaven at the top has shops, restaurants, and potentially snow in winter.

Key Details

  • Drive Start: Catalina Highway (from Tanque Verde area)
  • Summit Elevation: 9,157 feet
  • Road Conditions: Call 520-351-3351
  • Cost: FREE
  • Time Needed: 4-6 hours round trip
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 35 miles, 60 min to summit

Weather Considerations

  • Temperature 20-30Â°F cooler at top
  • Late December: Possible snow (or not - varies yearly)
  • Road may close after snowstorms
  • May require chains/4WD in snowy conditions

What Kids Will Love

  • IF SNOW: Sledding, snowball fights, playing in snow
  • Ski Valley (small ski area if conditions permit)
  • Mt. Lemmon General Store - fresh fudge!
  • Iron Door Restaurant - casual family dining

Pro Tips

  • Check road conditions morning-of: 520-351-3351
  • Fill gas tank before going (no gas at top)
  • Bring layers - much colder at summit
  • No snow? Still worth it for the views and climate change experience
