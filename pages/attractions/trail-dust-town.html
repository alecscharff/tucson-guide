Trail Dust Town

B-Tier | Old West Theme Park with FREE Admission

Home / Attractions / Trail Dust Town

Overview

Tier Rating: B-TIER (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 5/5 stars

A recreated 1880s Old West town with FREE admission! Wild West stunt shows, mini train, ferris wheel, gold panning, and shops. Evening-focused (opens 5 PM most attractions).

Key Details

  • Address: 6541 E Tanque Verde Rd, Tucson AZ 85715
  • Hours: Courtyard 8 AM - 11 PM | Rides/shows after 5 PM (Thu-Sun)
  • Cost: FREE admission, rides $3 each
  • Parking: FREE
  • Time Needed: 1-2 hours
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 8 miles, 15 min

Attractions

  • Pistoleros Wild West Show - Fri-Sun at 7 PM & 8 PM (gunfights, comedy, stunts!)
  • CP Huntington Railroad - mini train, 5-9 PM nightly
  • Circle-the-Wagons Ferris Wheel - Thu-Sun after 5 PM
  • Gold panning ($3), shooting gallery ($0.50)

What Kids Will Love (Ages 6 & 9)

  • Wild West stunt show is a huge hit with kids
  • Train ride through the town
  • Gold panning - keep what you find!
  • Old-timey photo ops

Pro Tips

  • Best visited evening (5-8 PM range)
  • Stunt show on Fri/Sat/Sun only
  • Pinnacle Peak Steakhouse on-site for adults wanting steak
