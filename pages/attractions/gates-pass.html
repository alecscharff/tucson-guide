Gates Pass Scenic Overlook

B-Tier | Best Sunset in Tucson

Home / Attractions / Gates Pass

Overview

Tier Rating: B-TIER (Good Addition)

Kid Appeal: 3/5 stars

A mountain pass in the Tucson Mountains with panoramic desert views. One of the best sunset spots in Tucson (USA Today "Best Place to Watch a Sunset" winner).

Recommended Stops (from NYT)

For diversity of landscapes and views, pair these two stops:

  1. First stop: David Yetman West Trailhead parking area - great desert views
  2. Second stop: Gates Pass Overlook Trailhead - panoramic sunset views

Key Details

  • Address: W Gates Pass Rd (at the summit)
  • Hours: 6 AM - 8 PM (gates lock at 8 PM!)
  • Cost: FREE
  • Parking: Two small lots at summit
  • Time Needed: 30 min - 1 hour
  • Distance from Rillito Park: 14 miles, 25 min
  • From Desert Museum: 4 miles, 5-10 min

December Sunset

  • Sunset time: ~5:20-5:25 PM
  • Arrive by 4:30-4:45 PM for parking
  • Gates close at 8 PM - cars CANNOT leave after

What Kids Will Love

  • Short hike up rocks for better views
  • Saguaro cactus photo ops everywhere
  • Quick visit, not a long commitment

Pro Tips

  • Best combined with Desert Museum (very close)
  • Visit Desert Museum, leave by 3:30-4 PM, catch sunset at Gates Pass
  • Limited parking - arrive early on nice evenings
  • Bring jackets - gets cool after sunset
