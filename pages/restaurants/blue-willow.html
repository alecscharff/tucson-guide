Blue Willow

Historic adobe home with one of Tucson's best patios

Home / Restaurants / Blue Willow

Vibe & Atmosphere

Set in a historic adobe home, Blue Willow offers a warm, "feel-good" atmosphere with a quirky gift shop perfect for browsing while you wait. The garden patio is lush and beautiful, providing a peaceful dining experience. It's a favorite for breakfast and brunch but serves comfort food all day.

For Kids

Picky: Excellent Excellent Health

Blue Willow is very family-friendly with solid options for picky eaters, even at dinner:

  • French Toast (Available for kids at dinner!)
  • Grilled Cheese Sandwiches
  • Bean Burritos
  • Pancakes (Breakfast)
  • Fresh baked goods and cookies

The kid-friendly menu and relaxed patio make it a stress-free choice for families.
