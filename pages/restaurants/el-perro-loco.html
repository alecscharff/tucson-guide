El Perro Loco

The "Crazy Dog" - Chipilon Style

Home / Restaurants / El Perro Loco

Vibe & Atmosphere

Known for their "Chipilon" style hot dogs with toasted bread and cheese. A popular food truck/stand with loyal local fans.

Excellent Health Picky: Moderate

For Kids

  • Quesadogo: Hot dog wrapped in tortilla with cheese (fun change!).
  • Regular Hot Dogs: Kid-friendly staple.

Key Details

  • Locations: 1285 W Ajo Way (West) | 3051 E 36th St (South)
  • Specialty: Quesadogo & Chipilon
