Seis Kitchen

Upscale street food, family atmosphere

Home / Restaurants / Seis Kitchen

Overview

Best for: Upscale street food, family atmosphere

The Vibe: Award-winning, scratch kitchen with large patios and heaters. Family-owned with extensive outdoor seating. Great for both breakfast and lunch/dinner.

Key Details

  • Locations: Mercado San Agustin (130 S Avenida del Convento) | River Rd (1765 E River Rd) | Tanque Verde (6530 E Tanque Verde Rd)
  • Hours: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM daily
  • Distance from Rillito Park: Varies by location
  • Health Score: Excellent

Menu for Kids

Kids Menu Items

  • Cheese quesadillas on handmade tortillas
  • Simple tacos

Picky Eater Rating: MODERATE

Quesadillas only - no American comfort foods like burgers or grilled cheese.

For Adults

  • Tacos from 6 regions of Mexico (fried avocado, carnitas, flank steak)
  • Tocino burrito (bacon, eggs, potatoes, cheese) for breakfast
  • Regional Mexican dishes with UNESCO Gastronomy designations
