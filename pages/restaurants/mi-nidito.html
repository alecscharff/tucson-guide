Mi Nidito

Famous Tucson Mexican - Serving since 1952

Home / Restaurants / Mi Nidito

Overview

Best for: Famous Tucson Mexican experience

The Vibe: Colorful, bustling "my little nest" - serving Tucson since 1952. In the heart of South 4th Ave, a historic Mexican-American neighborhood with murals and character. Worth the wait for the experience.

Key Details

  • Address: 1813 S 4th Ave, Tucson, AZ 85713
  • Hours: Wed-Thu 11 AM - 9:30 PM | Fri-Sat 11 AM - 11:30 PM | Sun 11 AM - 9:30 PM | Mon-Tue CLOSED
  • Distance from Rillito Park: South side location
  • Health Score: Excellent
  • Note: Can have waits - very popular!
