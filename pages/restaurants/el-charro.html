El Charro Cafe

Historic Tucson Experience Since 1922

Home / Restaurants / El Charro Cafe

The Vibe

Oldest Mexican restaurant in the US, run by the same family since 1922. Tourist-friendly but authentic. A true Tucson institution with traditional Sonoran cuisine.

Picky Eater: MODERATE Health Score: Excellent (Oro Valley) Mexican Historic

Kids Menu (Ninos)

All kids meals served with rice, beans, or fruit. Fries add $1.

  • Grilled Cheese Torta - $3.95 (Mexican-style grilled cheese)
  • Cheese Quesadilla
  • Beef Taco - $3.95
  • Ground Beef Nachos - $3.95
  • Bean Burro with Cheese - $3.95
  • Chicken Nuggets
  • Chicken Tenders

Kids Happy Hour Special!

Under 10 eat for $2.00! Amazing deal for families.

For Adults

  • Traditional Sonoran cuisine
  • Allegedly invented the chimichanga
  • Carne seca (dried beef, a specialty)
  • Authentic family recipes passed down for generations
