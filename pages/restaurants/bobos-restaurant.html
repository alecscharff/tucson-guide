Breakfast Top Pick

Bobo's Restaurant

Classic Diner - Best for Picky Eaters

The Vibe

Retro diner with booths, checker-tile floors, and UA murals. A Tucson institution with 80% regulars - you'll feel like a local. Cash-only, classic diner vibe in a strip mall that's easy to miss but worth finding.

Bobo's has been serving breakfast to Tucson locals since 1955. This is THE place for picky eaters with a full kids menu including grilled cheese, hamburgers, and french fries.

Essential Information
