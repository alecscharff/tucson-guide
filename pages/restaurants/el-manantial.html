El Manantial

Cash-only gem on 36th Street

Home / Restaurants / El Manantial

Vibe & Atmosphere

A true hidden gem on 36th Street. Famous for bacon-wrapped dogs with a jalapeño stuffed with cheese. Casual, outdoor seating, and very authentic.

Excellent Health Picky: Moderate

For Kids

  • Hot Dogs: Classic Sonoran style (ask for no jalapeño/veggies for kids).
  • Tacos: Carne asada and pollo options.

Key Details

  • Location: 953 E 36th St (Park & 36th)
  • Payment: CASH ONLY
  • Hours: Evenings are best
