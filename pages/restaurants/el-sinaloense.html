El Sinaloense

Authentic Sonoran Hot Dog Cart Experience

Home / Restaurants / El Sinaloense

Vibe & Atmosphere

Authentic Street Food: This isn't a sit-down restaurantâ€”it's a legendary food cart in a gravel lot on Alvernon Way. You eat outside under a metal canopy on picnic tables. It's a true Tucson experience.

Locals argue this might be the best Sonoran hot dog in town, even beating the bigger chains.

For Kids

Picky: Moderate Good Health

It's a hot dog stand! Most kids love it.

  • Sonoran Hot Dog: Bacon-wrapped, beans, mayo, mustard, tomatoes, onions. (Ask for it "plain" or "without beans/onions" for picky eaters).
  • Quesadillas: Often available.
  • Mexican Coke & Sodas: Glass bottles are a treat.

Key Details

  • Location: 1526 N Alvernon Way (Outdoor lot)
  • Hours: Evenings are best (usually open late)
  • Seating: Outdoor picnic tables only
