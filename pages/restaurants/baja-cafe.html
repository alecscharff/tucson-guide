Baja Cafe

Southwest breakfast + kid-approved pancakes

Home / Restaurants / Baja Cafe

Overview

Best for: Southwest breakfast + kid-approved pancakes

The Vibe: Casual, colorful Southwest decor with family-friendly atmosphere. Popular with locals and tourists alike. The Broadway location is larger; Campbell Ave feels more neighborhood-y.

Key Details

  • Locations: 7002 E Broadway Blvd | 2970 N Campbell Ave
  • Hours: 6:00 AM - 2:00 PM daily
  • Distance from Rillito Park: Varies by location
  • Health Score: Excellent
  • Wait times: Can be busy on weekends

Menu for Kids

Kids Menu Items

  • Grilled cheese - $6
  • Cheese quesadilla - $5
  • Famous cake/cookie-flavored pancakes

Picky Eater Rating: MODERATE

Has grilled cheese but no hamburger or fries. Good pancake options for kids who love breakfast foods.

For Adults

  • Huevos rancheros
  • Smoked meats
  • Homemade sauces
