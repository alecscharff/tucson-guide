La Reyna Hot Dogs

South 12th Avenue Classic

Home / Restaurants / La Reyna

Vibe & Atmosphere

Located on the famous South 12th Avenue "Best 23 Miles of Mexican Food" corridor. La Reyna is a classic, cash-only spot known for reasonable prices and authentic flavor.

  • Fries: Often available as a side.
