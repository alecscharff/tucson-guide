Eegee's

Local Institution Since 1971

Home / Restaurants / Eegee's

Overview

Best for: Quick, cheap, local institution

The Vibe: Fast food, local chain beloved by Tucson locals since 1971.

Key Details

  • Locations: 25+ locations around Tucson
  • Hours: Most locations 9:30 AM - 9:00/10:00 PM (varies by location)
  • Distance from Rillito Park: Multiple convenient locations
  • Health Score: Excellent

Menu for Kids

Kids Meals Include:

  • Kids eegee drink (strawberry, lemon, or pina colada)
  • Hot dogs
  • French fries or sugar cookie

Picky Eater Rating: LIMITED

Has fries and hot dogs only. No hamburgers or grilled cheese - subs and hot dogs are the main items.

For Adults

  • Handcrafted sub sandwiches
  • Signature ranch dressings
  • The famous "eegee" frozen fruit drink - a must-try!
