El Guero Canelo

James Beard Award Winner - Authentic Sonoran Hot Dogs

Home / Restaurants / El Guero Canelo

Overview

Best for: Authentic Sonoran hot dogs

The Vibe: Casual, quick-service with outdoor seating and lively atmosphere. Great when kids need food fast!

Key Details

  • Locations: 5201 S 12th Ave | 2480 N Oracle Rd | 5802 E 22nd St
  • Distance from Rillito Park: Varies by location
  • Health Score: Excellent
  • Awards: James Beard "America's Classics" Award 2018
  • Service: Quick service - perfect when kids are hungry!

Menu for Kids

Kids Menu Items

  • Cheese quesadillas
  • Bean and cheese burritos
  • Hot dogs
  • French fries

Picky Eater Rating: MODERATE

Has quesadillas, burritos, fries, and hot dogs. NO hamburgers available.

For Adults

  • The famous Sonoran dog (bacon-wrapped!)
  • Carne asada
  • Burritos
