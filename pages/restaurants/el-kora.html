El Kora Sonoran Hot Dogs

South Side staple for authentic dogs

Home / Restaurants / El Kora

Vibe & Atmosphere

Located deep on South 6th Avenue, El Kora is known for the "Chucho Dog" and authentic vibes. It's a casual spot perfect for a quick bite if you are on the south side.

Excellent Health Picky: Moderate

For Kids

  • Hot Dogs: Classic kid favorite.
  • Quesadillas: Cheese crisp options usually available.

Key Details

  • Location: 6028 S 6th Ave
  • Specialty: Chucho Dog
