Pinnacle Peak Steakhouse

Famous "No Ties Allowed" steakhouse in Trail Dust Town

Home / Restaurants / Pinnacle Peak

Vibe & Atmosphere

Located within the Western-themed Trail Dust Town, Pinnacle Peak is a Tucson icon famous for its "No Ties Allowed" policyâ€”wear one and they'll cut it off! The atmosphere is rowdy, fun, and authentically Old West, with long communal tables and sawdust on the floors. It's an experience as much as a meal.

Before or after dinner, families can explore Trail Dust Town, which features a narrow-gauge train, Ferris wheel, carousel, and stunt shows.

For Kids

Picky: Good Excellent Health

The Lil' Cowboy/Cowgirl menu (ages 10 and under) covers all the bases:

  • Lil' Cowboy Steak (smaller cut for kids)
  • Hamburgers and Hot Dogs
  • Cheesy Quesadillas
  • BBQ Baby Back Ribs
  • Grilled Chicken

Meals typically come with a side and drink. The environment is loud and kid-friendly, so you don't have to worry about noise.
