Tucson Gardens

Beautiful oases in the desert - perfect for family strolls

Tucson offers surprising lushness if you know where to look. From butterfly pavilions to historic orchards, these gardens are rated for their kid-friendliness.

Garden Attractions

Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Zoo + Gardens

Zoo, aquarium & gardens. Includes mountain lions & caves. The ultimate desert garden experience.

5/5 Stars ~$100 Family
